Scrambled Eggs

Description

It's scrambled eggs

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Put pan on heat
  2. Crack eggs in bowl
  3. Pour a little bit of milk in bowl
  4. Whisk eggs and milk in bowl with fork
  5. Put butter in pan
  6. Wait for butter to melt then put egg mixture in pan
  7. Cook it until it's done
